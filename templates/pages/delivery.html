Frequently Asked Questions

Orders placed before 4pm will be shipped same day. Normal delivery takes 3-5 days. Express delivery takes 24-48h after payment confirmation.

We offer FREE tracked shipping worldwide, no matter which country you’re from.

Customs fees may apply depending on your country. Please check with your local customs office for more information.

You can send us an email after 5 days of your order if you haven’t received it. We will then provide you with a tracking number.

If you are unhappy with your purchase, contact us within 30 days of receiving your order for a return address and refund instructions.
